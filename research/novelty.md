# Novelty of Proposed Work

Existing atomic clock systems focus on:

* Stability measurement
* Frequency control
* Environmental compensation

Limitations:

* Lack of physical sensitivity attribution
* Limited predictive maintenance capability
* Absence of digital twin integration
* No unified health monitoring platform

Proposed Contribution:

1. Physics-informed Digital Twin for Rubidium and Cesium clocks.
2. AI-based Future Drift Prediction.
3. One-Class SVM Anomaly Detection.
4. Physical sensitivity attribution using measured parameter deviations.
5. Predictive Maintenance Recommendation Engine.
6. Unified Dashboard integrating all modules.

Expected Outcome:

A complete AI-driven Atomic Clock Health Monitoring Platform capable of predicting instability, identifying root causes, and supporting maintenance decisions.
